Stress index — averaging vs PCA

Two ways to collapse the five z-scored credit components into one headline mark. z-score average is the current published method (inverse-vol-weighted, fully causal); PCA projects onto the first principal component (data-driven weights, scale-matched to the average, not causal). The right axis (%/yr) shows the economic net yield — (interest − losses)/balance, no z-scoring needed — in two flavors: cash (violet, actual interest collected from the tapes — the tradeable "price" series) and accrued (green dashed, coupon × balance, what the pool would earn if everyone paid).
